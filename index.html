About Spam Clean

The Problem:

Spam has been a problem for many decades. But unlike the virus, not all organizations are investing in their cyber defense to effectively block them from their network explicitly. Thus creating a gap for them to increase their activity.

The Solution:

To solve this evolving problem we team have developed a spam cleaner. It is completely automated and safe. This is a web app using the Gmail API to automatically and permanently delete SPAM and/or TRASH from your Gmail folders at any interval you want to set. Normally these folders delete every 30 days but you can change that to every minute or every day or however often you like.
